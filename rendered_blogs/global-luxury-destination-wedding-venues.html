Destinations

1. Villa d'Este, Lake Como, Italy: Renaissance Romance on the Water

The Epitome of European Aristocracy

Set on the shores of Lake Como, Villa d'Este is a former 16th-century royal residence that has been transformed into one of the world's most iconic luxury hotels. Known for its immaculate Renaissance gardens, historic mosaic pillars, and the legendary floating pool, it is the absolute gold standard for a romantic European wedding. Saying your vows here feels like stepping straight into a classic painting, surrounded by towering cypress trees and the gentle ripple of the lake.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

The hotel's 25-acre gardens offer a variety of ceremony settings. The most famous is the Mosaic Garden, featuring a double-arched nymph temple decorated with intricate stone mosaics and statues. For the reception, the grand ballroom—adorned with crystal chandeliers, frescoed ceilings, and antique oil paintings—sets the stage for an opulent banquet. Anticipate buyout and execution costs starting at $150,000 to $300,000+ USD, depending on guest count and customization.

Unmissable Couple Detail: Arrival by Riva Boat

Make a dramatic entrance to your reception by arriving on a vintage mahogany Riva speedboat. Cruise along the shoreline of Lake Como with your photographer, capturing the late-afternoon sun hitting the pastel-hued facades of the lakeside villas before stepping onto the Villa d'Este private pier.

2. Amanpuri, Phuket, Thailand: Zen-Like Seclusion in a Coconut Grove

Barefoot Luxury on a Private Peninsula

Overlooking the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea, Amanpuri—the flagship property of the legendary Aman resorts—offers a serene, ultra-exclusive wedding setting. Tucked away in a mature coconut plantation on a private peninsula in Phuket, the resort is designed to reflect traditional Ayutthayan architecture. It is a venue where minimalist luxury meets warm Thai hospitality, perfect for couples seeking a refined, spiritually grounded beach celebration.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

Host an intimate ceremony on the resort's private, crescent-shaped white sand beach. With the Andaman Sea as your altar and the sunset casting a warm gold glow over the sand, it is the definition of beachfront elegance. Receptions are held around the dramatic midnight-blue swimming pool, surrounded by stone steps and burning torches. Venue fees and villa buyouts start around $80,000 USD for intimate groups.

Unmissable Couple Detail: Lantern Release

At the end of your reception, join your guests on the beach to release traditional floating lanterns into the warm night air, making a wish together as a constellation of lights drifts out over the ocean.

3. One&Only Reethi Rah, Maldives: Overwater Tropical Elegance

Absolute Seclusion in a Maldivian Sanctuary

For the couple who dreams of a tropical paradise, the **One&Only Reethi Rah** in the Maldives is unmatched. This private island sanctuary features 12 pristine white sand beaches and ultra-luxury villas nestled along the lagoon. A wedding here is the ultimate castaway fantasy, elevated by top-tier gastronomy, personalized wedding planning, and absolute privacy.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

The resort features a stunning **Wedding Pavilion**, an open-air structure built on stilts over the water, featuring a glass-bottom aisle and panoramic views of the Indian Ocean. Alternatively, you can say your vows on a private sandbank accessible only by yacht, surrounded by nothing but pure white sand and shallow turquoise waters. Custom wedding packages start at approximately $15,000 USD, rising to full-island buyouts exceeding $500,000 USD.

Unmissable Couple Detail: Sandbank Dinner

Celebrate your marriage the evening after your wedding with a private candlelight dinner on a deserted sandbank. Dine on fresh grilled seafood under a blanket of stars, completely isolated in the middle of the Indian Ocean.

Practicalities

Practicalities for Couples

    Legal Requirements

    In many destination wedding countries (such as Italy and Thailand), there are specific legal residency and paperwork requirements. Many couples choose to complete the legal paperwork in their home country first, hosting a symbolic ceremony at their destination venue.

    Guest Experience

    Select a venue that offers a variety of guest accommodations and is accessible via a major international airport hub. Provide guests with a detailed itinerary of pre- and post-wedding activities.

    Planning Timeline

    Secure your date and venue at least 12 to 18 months in advance, as these premium global properties book out fast.
