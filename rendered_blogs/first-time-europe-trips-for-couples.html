The Old World Grandeur: Crafting Your First European Adventure

There is a reason Europe remains the ultimate destination for romantic travel. It is a continent where history is written in stone, where every street corner reveals a historic café or a scenic view, and where a short train ride can transport you to a completely different language, culture, and culinary tradition. For couples planning their first trip to Europe, the challenge is not finding beautiful places to visit—it is choosing between them to create a trip that is relaxing, romantic, and memorable.

Rather than trying to rush through ten countries in ten days, the secret to a successful first-time European trip is to select a few iconic, high-romance destinations and slow down. By focusing on a classic route like Paris, Venice, and the Amalfi Coast, you will experience the perfect balance of palatial romance, historic water channels, and dramatic coastal beauty. Here is our couple's guide to your first European Grand Tour.

Accommodations

Accommodations: Historic Palaces & Cliffside Luxury

    ◆The Splurge
    Le Sirenuse, Positano (Amalfi Coast, Italy)

    A legendary luxury hotel perched high on the cliffs of Positano, featuring a beautiful red facade and terraces overlooking the blue sea. The hotel's restaurant is lit entirely by 400 candles at night, creating the ultimate romantic dinner setting.

    Best room: Junior Suite Ocean View
    Price range: $1,200 - $2,500 USD per night
    ◆The Romantic Sweet Spot
    Hotel Danieli, Venice (Italy)

    A historic 14th-century palace hotel located just steps from St. Mark's Square. The hotel features grand marble columns, antique chandeliers, and a rooftop terrace restaurant overlooking the Venetian lagoon.

    Best room: Premium Double Room
    Price range: $600 - $1,000 USD per night
Gastronomy

Gastronomy: Romantic Dinners

Candlelit dinner at La Sponda (Positano)
The Unmissable Splurge

Located at Le Sirenuse, La Sponda is one of the most romantic restaurants in the world. As the sun sets, the staff lights 400 candles throughout the restaurant. Enjoy a menu of fresh Mediterranean dishes, accompanied by live mandolin music and views of the Positano cliffs glowing in the dark.

Destinations

Iconic Couple Stopovers

    1. The Seine Bridges at Night (Paris, France)

    Stroll along the Pont Alexandre III at night. As the Eiffel Tower sparkles in the background and the street lamps glow, it is one of the most romantic places in the world. Optimal time: 10:00 PM when the tower sparkles.

    The Grand Canal (Venice, Italy)

    Rent a private wooden water launch at sunset. Cruise down the Grand Canal, watching the historic palaces reflect on the quiet water.

    The Cliffs of Positano (Amalfi Coast, Italy)

    Walk the narrow roads lined with bougainvillea, stopping at roadside stands for fresh lemon granita and looking down at the colorful houses.
