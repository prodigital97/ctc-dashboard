Beachfront Sophistication: Choosing Your Oceanfront Altar

For couples who live by the tides, there is no setting more romantic for marriage than the ocean. The sound of the surf, the warmth of the sand, and the endless horizon create an atmosphere of pure freedom. However, a luxury beach wedding goes far beyond a simple arch on public sand. It is about private peninsulas, exclusive beach clubs, and remote private islands where every detail—from the floral arches to the sea-to-table cuisine—is executed to perfection.

Destinations

1. Cheval Blanc St-Barth Isle de France, St. Barthélemy: Chic Caribbean Romance

French Glamour Meets Caribbean Beauty

Set on the prestigious Flamands Beach in St. Barthélemy, Cheval Blanc St-Barth is the epitome of French West Indies sophistication. Designed by Jacques Grange, the property combines colonial charm with contemporary French style. For couples, it offers a wedding setting that is chic, fashionable, and deeply romantic, surrounded by azure waters and the glamorous atmosphere of St. Barts.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

Say your vows on the soft white sands of Flamands Beach, with the turquoise Caribbean Sea as your backdrop. Receptions are held at **La Case**, the resort's signature restaurant designed by Jean Imbert, offering a refined menu of Caribbean-inspired French cuisine. Celebrate with champagne toasts around the beachfront pool under a canopy of palms. Buyout and wedding costs start around $100,000 to $250,000+ USD, depending on guest count and season.

Unmissable Couple Detail: Sunset Catamaran Toast

The evening before your wedding, host a private sunset catamaran cruise for your guests. Sail along the rugged coastline of St. Barts, sipping French champagne and enjoying local oysters as the sun drops behind the horizon.

2. Kokomo Private Island, Fiji: South Pacific Sanctuary

Absolute Seclusion in a Marine Reserve

For couples seeking true privacy and wild tropical beauty, Kokomo Private Island in Fiji is unmatched. Located in the Kadavu island group, Kokomo is surrounded by the Great Astrolabe Reef, the fourth largest reef in the world. A wedding here is the ultimate private island escape, where traditional Fijian warmth meets ultra-luxury villas, organic dining, and pristine marine reefs.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

Say your vows on **Walker Beach**, a secluded stretch of sand flanked by lush jungle and calm turquoise waters. Receptions are held in the open-air **Beach House**, featuring a menu designed around the island's organic farm and fresh catches. The celebration can conclude with a traditional Fijian fire-lighting ceremony and local choir music. Full-island buyouts start around $120,000 USD per night (generally requiring a 3 to 5-night minimum).

Unmissable Couple Detail: Private Reef Dive

Celebrate your marriage with a private couple's scuba dive on the Great Astrolabe Reef. Swim alongside manta rays, sea turtles, and vibrant coral gardens, capturing the moment with an underwater photographer.

3. Gili Lankanfushi, Maldives: Eco-Luxury Sanctuary

Barefoot Luxury and Overwater Sanctuaries

Located in the North Malé Atoll, Gili Lankanfushi is an award-winning eco-luxury resort that champions barefoot romance. The resort's philosophy of 'No News, No Shoes' encourages couples to disconnect from the world and connect with nature. With its iconic overwater villas, private sandbanks, and commitment to marine conservation, it is the perfect tropical setting for an intimate, sustainable luxury wedding.

The Perfect Wedding Setup

Exchange vows on a private, isolated sandbank in the middle of the turquoise lagoon. The crew sets up a simple, elegant bamboo arch decorated with local tropical flowers. Receptions are held at the **Overwater Grill**, featuring fresh seafood barbecues and feet-in-the-sand dining under the stars. Custom wedding packages start at approximately $12,000 USD.

Unmissable Couple Detail: Sleep Under the Stars

Spend your wedding night in the resort's Private Reserve—the largest overwater villa in the world. The villa features an outdoor rooftop daybed where you can sleep under a canopy of stars, surrounded only by the soft lapping of the lagoon.

Practicalities

Practicalities for Couples

    Legal Paperwork

    Many couples choose to hold a symbolic ceremony at their destination venue, completing the legal wedding registration in their home country to avoid complex international bureaucracy.

    Travel Logistics for Guests

    St. Barts is accessible via small charter flights from St. Maarten (SXM). Kokomo is accessed via a private helicopter or seaplane transfer from Nadi (NAN). Ensure guest travel guides are detailed and booked early.

    Climate Seasonality

    The Caribbean dry season is December to April. Fiji is best from May to October. Maldives is dry from November to April. Align your wedding date with the destination's dry season.
