Destinations

1. Sandals Royal Barbados (Bridgetown, Barbados)

The Caribbean's Most Sophisticated Couples All-Inclusive

Sandals Royal Barbados is the flagship of the Sandals portfolio—targeting an older, more sophisticated couple. The resort feels far more like a boutique hotel than the typical sprawling all-inclusive complex. The architecture is colonial British Caribbean, with white balustrades, mahogany furniture, and mature tropical gardens. It sits alongside its sister property, Sandals Barbados, offering guests access to twice the restaurants, bars, and beach space.

Why It Works for NYC Couples

    Flight

    JetBlue and American Airlines offer nonstop flights from JFK to Barbados (BGI) in approximately 4.5 hours.

    The Inclusion

    All meals across 9 restaurants (including a rooftop Sky Pool Bar), all spirits, butler service in premium categories, water sports, diving, and transfers are included. No bill at checkout.

    The Splurge

    The Millionaire Butler Suites feature private plunge pools overlooking the beach, 24/7 butler service, and a private dining setup on your villa terrace.

Rates: From $650 to $1,500 USD per person per night for premium suite categories (all-inclusive).

Destinations

2. Excellence Playa Mujeres (Cancun Area, Mexico)

Adults-Only Sophistication on the Mexican Riviera

Located on a private peninsula north of Cancun's hotel zone, Excellence Playa Mujeres is strictly adults-only and markets itself as a 'refined' alternative to the typical Cancun package resort. The architecture is Mexican colonial, with terracotta roofs, infinity pools, and lush tropical gardens leading down to a pristine, uncrowded beach on the lagoon side.

Why It Works for NYC Couples

    Flight

    Multiple airlines including Delta, United, JetBlue, and American offer nonstop flights from JFK and EWR to Cancun (CUN) in approximately 3.5 hours.

    The Inclusion

    11 gourmet specialty restaurants (French, Japanese, Mexican, Italian, and Mediterranean), premium spirits, butler service (Excellence Club tier), and a full spa credit are included.

    The Splurge

    The Excellence Club Swim-Out Suite gives you direct step-out access to a heated swim-out pool from your own terrace, plus exclusive access to the Excellence Club lounge, beach area, and à la carte service.

Rates: From $450 to $900 USD per person per night for Excellence Club categories (all-inclusive).

3. Half Moon, Rose Hall, Jamaica

Historic Jamaican Luxury with Modern Elegance

Half Moon is a grand, 400-acre Jamaican estate resort—a legacy property with a history dating back to 1954, set on a crescent-shaped private beach near Montego Bay. Unlike the newer cookie-cutter all-inclusive properties, Half Moon offers a more refined, estate-style atmosphere, with individual cottages, private villas with personal pools, and a world-class equestrian center. It recently completed a significant renovation, blending its colonial heritage with contemporary luxury design.

Why It Works for NYC Couples

    Flight

    American, Delta, JetBlue, and Spirit offer nonstop flights from JFK and EWR to Montego Bay (MBJ) in approximately 3.5 hours.

    The Inclusion

    Half Moon offers both all-inclusive and modified meal plan packages. For the full AI experience, select the 'Jamaican Discovery' plan, which covers breakfast, lunch, dinner, and premium spirits.

    The Splurge

    A private villa with your own pool, a dedicated butler, and in-villa chef services. Ideal for an ultra-private honeymoon week.

Rates: From $500 to $1,200 USD per person per night (all-inclusive packages).

4. Belmond Cap Juluca, Anguilla

The Hidden Gem: Moorish Architecture on the Caribbean's Finest Beach

Anguilla is one of the Caribbean's best-kept secrets—a tiny, flat island known for the finest beaches in the region and an uncharacteristically sophisticated tourism scene. Belmond Cap Juluca is the island's landmark resort: a string of white domed, Moorish-inspired villas strung along the ivory sands of Maunday's Bay. It is serene, intimate, and definitively romantic.

Why It Works for NYC Couples

    Flight

    American Airlines offers nonstop flights from JFK to St. Maarten/St. Martin (SXM) in approximately 3.5 hours, with a 20-minute ferry to Anguilla. Delta and United also offer nonstop options.

    The Stay

    Cap Juluca operates on a MAP (Modified American Plan) rather than a true all-inclusive, covering breakfast and dinner. The level of exclusivity and the quality of the beach more than justify the premium.

Rates: From $700 to $3,000+ USD per night (breakfast and dinner included).

    1. Millionaire Suite Rooftop Plunge Pool (Sandals Royal Barbados)

    Position the camera low, shooting across the surface of the rooftop plunge pool toward the horizon, with the Caribbean sea framed beyond. Optimal time: Sunrise for a completely calm, mirror-flat pool surface.

    Maunday's Bay Shoreline (Belmond Cap Juluca)

    Photograph the white Moorish domes of Cap Juluca from the far western end of the beach, using a wide-angle lens to capture the full sweep of the crescent bay with the resort in the background.

    2. Swim-Out Pool at Sunset (Excellence Playa Mujeres)

    Shoot from within the swim-out pool looking toward the main infinity pool and the ocean, capturing the cascading layers of water and light.

Practicalities

Practicalities for Couples

    Best Value Season

    The Caribbean shoulder season (May to June, and November) offers the best rates while maintaining excellent weather. Avoid the peak hurricane months of September and October.

    Booking Strategy

    Book at least 3 to 4 months in advance for winter travel (December to April). Many resorts offer early-bird promotions with complimentary room upgrades.

    Travel Insurance

    Always purchase travel insurance that includes hurricane cancellation coverage for Caribbean trips.
